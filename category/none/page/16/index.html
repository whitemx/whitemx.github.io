Finished Two Books

After about of “worthy” reading earlier in the year I am back to trash this week. I get through it a lot quicker and so this week have finished two books.

Prey by Michael Crichton is sub-standard airport fodder. The writing was terrible. You increasingly get the impression from his books that they are less a novel and more a set of ideas for a movie or TV show. I suppose I only encourage him by buying the books so I must stop that.

In contrast DaveGorman’s Googlewhack Adventure is great. He was trying for a novel but ended up with another classic non-fiction comedy. The only problem with his books is that I embarrass myself on the train by laughing too much. However, compared to his eccentricities I don’t have too much to worry about.
